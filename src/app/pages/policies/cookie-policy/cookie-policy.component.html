By using the Platform, you consent to the use of cookies unless you disable them in your browser. This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They allow essential platform functions such as secure login, remembering device preferences, and improving file upload performance.

  • Session Cookies: Removed when the browser is closed.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored until manually deleted or expired.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose
Essential Cookies Enable login, file access, session security, and core platform operations.
Performance Cookies Improve file upload speed, syncing, caching, and platform responsiveness.
Analytics Cookies Provide anonymous usage insights such as storage activity, errors, performance metrics. (Example: privacy-focused analytics such as Matomo).
Security Cookies Detect unusual login attempts, prevent fraud, and secure user accounts.
